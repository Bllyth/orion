5th Anniversary and Courtesy Visit by Hon. Martin Moshisho Martine, Deputy Governor ,Kajiado County

On the 10th of July 2020, our institution both in Kandisi celebrated its 5th Anniversary since its opening where we have been able to provide essential services of education , rehabilitation and training to children and youth with intellectual disabilities.

Our commitment remains the same â€“ Promotion of human dignity through loving, respecting and caring for the most vulnerable members of our communities.

This time round , our celebrations were unique and special owing to the current pandemic ,nevertheless we celebrated with our local community -300 children who are beneficiaries of our on-going daily feeding program.

Additionally, we received a surprise visit by the Deputy Governor of Kajiado County Hon. Martin Moshisho Martine and his delegation who joined in the celebrations and volunteered to serve the children lunch and goodies.

Special gratitude goes to all our donors, friends and benefactors who have made our work and interventions possible throughout the years.
