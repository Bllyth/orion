Graduation ceremony 2018

On Tuesday, 6th November 2018 we one of the most important and significant event in the life of our Centre here in Kenya

16 of our youth graduated after 3 years of life skills training and are now employed in various capacities in the Centre.

Additionally, with the help of Strathmore University Scholl of Law, each of them is accompanied by a mentor who is a Law Student. This is meant to help them and protect them from any form of abuse and also help the mentors in seeing the reality the disabilities situation in Kenya.

As result, we can now see them as responsible and independent adults with a clear conscience about their rights and responsibilities.

This joy and happiness belongs to the many people who have trusted in our work.
