Community Inclusion

An important facet is the education of parents, relatives and community to facilitate the process of integration and inclusion of the children and youth in their social community. To involve the community, families of children attending the school put in work hours by helping attend to the children, building, and upkeep of the Centre. This has resulted in the communities becoming vested in the Centre. It also provides the necessary contact with parents and extended family to provide training and support. The Centre is also used for community events thus becoming an integral part of the community at large.
